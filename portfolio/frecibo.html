Startup

Frecibo

Date: Jun 2017

Team members:

Frecibo was the first startup I got involved in. It is a platform, aimed at students allowing them to order home cooked food from to campus. The initiative was to provide students with cheaper and healthier food options as well as giving local and aspiring chefs the oppurtunity to get paid for cooking. I am responsible for the design and front-end development of the platform as well as the majority of marketing material. The site has been built on PHP, with styling in SASS.
