Branding + Website

Fire Door Inspection Solutions UK

Date: 2016

Fire Door Inspection SOlutions UK (FDISuk) are a company offering a range of Fire Door services. They were one of my first big clients in which I was given almost a blank canvas to come up with their first brand and website. The logo makes a play with the letter 'F' in Fire and a door and was made in Adobe Illustrator. The Website was made in Pug, Sass, jQuery and Gulp and desiged using Sketch.
