Startup

Thirty Degress

Team Members:

Thirty Degrees is a Digital Agency specialising in the design and development of bespoke, mobile responsive web applications.

The company was formed by myself and fellow university colleagues in June 2017 in order to take on web development projects of a larger scale compared to those we had undertaken in a sole freelance capacity. As well as being a co-founder and director of Thirty Degrees, I take on the role of a designer and front-end developer in the projects we partake in.

Open Source Project

Eden

Eden is a front-end web framework focused on beautiful design and unique web development.

It was developed as my 3rd year dissertation project at The University of Warwick where its main purpose is to easily allow users to design, build and develop beautiful looking websites that are unlike any other. Front-end frameworks such as Eden provide the base and foundation to start your web project.

Date

August 2016 - today

Visit Website
Homepage

Homepage giving a brief introduction to what the framework is about, examples of websites it has been used in and also brands that use it. Designed as a clean UI with options easy at hand. Features a centralised horizontal navigation bar at the top of the page.

Get Started & About

Follows a similar clean design to the homepage with the same navigation bar and hero image at the top of each page. Content then follows underneath.

Fairpay

Fairpay is a web application designed to help students keep track of their bills when moving into their new off campus accomodation. This was a coursework project for the Web Development Technologies module during my first year of University. I designed both the logo behind the project and the platform itself before developing it in HTML, CSS, PHP, JavaScript, JQuery, AJAX and SQL.
